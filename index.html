About Me

Seila is originally from Jacksonville, FL. He initially studied computer science and changed his major and graduated with a Finance degree from University of North Florida. He started his career in the finance industry as a trader at Fidelity Investments in 2007 and worked for various companies since then.

He still felt that something was missing and was not happy in his career. He decided to go back to his roots in programming and expect to start again with his original passion for coding.
